About

iQuHACK (Interdisciplinary Quantum Hackathon) is MIT's first annual quantum hackathon. We aim to bring people from a diverse set of backgrounds, including physics, computer science, chemistry, and even arts together to explore improvements and applications of near-term quantum devices.
